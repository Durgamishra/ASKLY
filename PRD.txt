# Askly – AI Conversational Assistant

**Version:** 1.0

**Category:** AI-Powered Conversational Assistant
**Platform:** Web, Mobile (iOS & Android), Desktop
**Prepared By:** Product Team
**Status:** Product Vision & MVP Specification

---

# 1. Executive Summary

Askly is an advanced AI-powered conversational platform designed to provide intelligent, human-like interactions for users across personal, educational, professional, and creative use cases. Built on state-of-the-art language models, Askly serves as a smart assistant capable of answering questions, generating content, solving problems, assisting with coding, conducting research, and providing personalized recommendations.

The goal of Askly is to become the user's primary AI companion—faster, smarter, and more intuitive than traditional search engines and digital assistants.

---

# 2. Product Vision

To empower every individual and business with an intelligent AI assistant that delivers instant knowledge, creativity, productivity, and problem-solving capabilities through natural conversation.

---

# 3. Problem Statement

Current information discovery tools require users to:

* Search through multiple websites
* Read lengthy articles
* Switch between apps for different tasks
* Spend significant time finding accurate answers

Users need a single AI assistant that can:

* Understand natural language
* Deliver instant responses
* Generate content
* Solve complex tasks
* Learn user preferences
* Operate across multiple domains

Askly addresses these challenges through a unified conversational AI experience.
